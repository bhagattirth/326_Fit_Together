UFit

Ufit is the ultimate workout partner finder for students at UMass. With Ufit, you can search for people who have similar fitness goals and interests, and connect with them to get fit together at the Rec! Whether you're looking for a running buddy, someone to lift weights with, or just someone to sweat it out with, Ufit is here to help you find your perfect match.

Rating System

Our app's 1-5 rating system is designed to give users an easy way to rate the partners they work out with. The system allows users to select a rating for their partner from one to five stars. Once a user has been rated, the overall rating on that user will be visible on their profile for all to see.

How It Works

The app helps people find a workout partner at the Rec by allowing them to match with other users who are interested in working out. The app also allows users to connect with other users who have a lot of similarities and think they would work well together. After working out with someone you can rate them on a scale of 1-5.
